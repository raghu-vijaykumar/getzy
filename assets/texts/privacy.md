# Getzy Privacy Policy
Your privacy is important to us. Getzy is designed to be a privacy-first application.

## Data Collection
Getzy does NOT collect any personal data. No analytics, no crash reporting, and no telemetry are included in this version.

## Local Storage
All torrent metadata, settings, and RSS feed information are stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to any external server except for the necessary communication with torrent peers and trackers.

## Network Access
Getzy requires internet access to connect to peers and trackers to download torrent data.
